Aims & Scope

The Oracle of Medicine and Medical Sciences is a bimonthly peer-reviewed open-access journal that publishes preclinical and clinical research of global significance across all medical disciplines, enabling clinicians and other researchers to learn about developments and innovations both within and across these disciplines.

We accept manuscripts in the following areas: Anaesthesia, Anatomy, Biochemistry, Cancer Research, Cardiology, Dentistry, Dermatology, Diabetes, Endocrinology, and more.
